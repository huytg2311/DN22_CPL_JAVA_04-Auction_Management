II. General rules

General definition:

The seller is the site owner.
Buyers are Vietnamese citizens across 63 provinces. The auctioneer has the right to register an account to make transactions.

III. Transaction Process

For auctioneers at smartAuction.com website

Step 1: Users need to register an account and deposit an amount in their wallet with enough money for auction (User can withdraw whenever they want).
Step 2: Find the product to be auctioned.
Step 3: View the price and details of that product, if you agree to bid, you need to enter the amount you bid (During the auction is taking place) and the amount in your wallet is satisfied (the amount in the wallet must be greater than the auction amount).
Step 4: At the end of the auction, if the user who is the highest bidder during the auction period is the one who has the product and can see the details in the user's cart. If you own a "smartAuction.com Gift Voucher", you scratch the silver layer on the card and enter "Discount Code" in the "Discount Code" box to be deducted directly from the order.
Step 5: After receiving the buyer's order, smartAuction.com will contact the customer through the phone number you provide to verify the order information.
Step 6: SmartAuction.com delivers goods to customers' homes or customers come directly to the facility to receive goods.

Shipping process:

smartAuction.com delivers nationwide. When receiving an order from the buyer and after confirming the purchase information by phone, faucions.com will proceed to deliver the goods at the request of the customer. IV. Payment Process

+ Step 1: The auctioneer learns about the advertised products and services.
+ Step 2: After registering an account, the auctioneer will be provided with a wallet address.
+ Step 3: The successful bidder will automatically enter the wallet account.
